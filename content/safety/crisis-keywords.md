# Crisis Keywords

> **STARTER EXAMPLE — written by the developer.**
> SME, the list below is a baseline of common signals in English.
> Please add or remove based on the population, languages, and
> idioms **your** users actually use. Each phrase is matched
> **case-insensitively** as a substring of the user's message.

## Self-harm and suicide signals

- kill myself
- killing myself
- end my life
- end it all
- want to die
- don't want to be alive
- suicide
- suicidal
- hurt myself
- harm myself
- self harm
- self-harm
- cutting myself
- overdose

## Immediate danger to others

- hurt someone
- kill someone
- attack
- shoot
- abuse

## Acute crisis indicators

- emergency
- can't breathe
- panic attack
- in danger
- unsafe at home
- being abused

> **Important:** False positives are acceptable here. It is much
> better for Willow to surface the crisis banner once unnecessarily
> than to miss a real signal. Err on the side of including a phrase
> if you're unsure.
