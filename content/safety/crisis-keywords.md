# Crisis Keywords

> SME, this file lists **words and phrases** that should trigger
> Willow's crisis response (a visible banner with hotlines, plus a
> shift in the AI's tone toward grounding and resource-sharing).
>
> Add words on a single line each, under the appropriate section. They
> are matched **case-insensitively**. Keep them short — exact phrases
> are matched as substrings of the user's message.

## Self-harm and suicide signals

- kill myself
- killing myself
- end my life
- end it all
- want to die
- don't want to be alive
- suicide
- suicidal
- hurt myself
- harm myself
- self harm
- self-harm
- cutting myself
- overdose

## Immediate danger to others

- hurt someone
- kill someone
- attack
- shoot
- abuse

## Acute crisis indicators

- emergency
- can't breathe
- panic attack
- in danger
- unsafe at home
- being abused

> **Important:** False positives are acceptable here. It is much
> better for Willow to surface the crisis banner once unnecessarily
> than to miss a real signal. Err on the side of including a phrase
> if you're unsure.
