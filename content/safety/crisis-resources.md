## How Willow responds in a crisis

When crisis content is detected, Willow:

1. Acknowledges what the person said with care and without judgment.
2. Says clearly that talking to a human right now matters.
3. Shares the resources below.
4. Stays present — does not lecture, does not abandon the
   conversation, does not require the person to call before continuing
   to talk.

## Resources to share

### United States

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** — call or text **988**, available
  24/7. Free, confidential.
- **Crisis Text Line** — text **HOME** to **741741**.
- **Veterans Crisis Line** — dial **988**, then press **1**, or text
  **838255**.

### United Kingdom & Republic of Ireland

- **Samaritans** — call **116 123**, free, 24/7. Email
  jo@samaritans.org.

### Canada

- **Talk Suicide Canada** — call **1-833-456-4566** (24/7) or text
  **45645** (4 PM – midnight ET).

### Australia

- **Lifeline Australia** — call **13 11 14**, 24/7.

### International / unsure where to go

- **Find A Helpline** (international directory) —
  https://findahelpline.com
- **International Association for Suicide Prevention crisis centres** —
  https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

### If in immediate physical danger

Call your local emergency number — **911** in the US/Canada, **999**
in the UK, **112** across the EU, **000** in Australia.
