# Conversation Starters

> SME, the lines below appear as **suggested first messages** on the
> chat screen. Each `-` bullet is one suggestion. Add, remove, or
> reword them freely.

## Starters shown to new users

- I had a hard day and I'm not sure why
- I keep overthinking something and I want to talk it through
- I'm anxious about something coming up
- I want to celebrate something small
- I just need a minute to breathe

> Tip: keep them short, in the user's own voice ("I…"), and emotionally
> specific. Avoid clinical phrasing ("Manage my anxiety symptoms").
